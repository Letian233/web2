French Snails

Snails have been a source of easily accessible protein since ancient Roman times. This culinary tradition was passed down through Europe, and the French have known the delicacy of snails for centuries, preparing various dishes with them.During the Middle Ages, snails were a food source for the common people due to poverty.

Tiramisu

The Soldier and His Wife: A widely circulated story is that Tiramisu originated during World War II. An Italian soldier was about to go to war, and his wife, wanting to prepare provisions for him, used all the biscuits and bread in their home to create a single dessert for him to take along. This dessert was Tiramisu.
